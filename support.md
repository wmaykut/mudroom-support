# Support — The Mud Room

**The Mud Room** is a supportive, accountability-oriented community for creatives to
share works-in-progress and keep momentum. This page explains how to get help, report a
problem, or request a feature.

If you just need a fast answer, check the [FAQ](#faq) first!
---

## How to get help

| I want to… | Do this |
|---|---|
| **Report a bug** | [Open a bug report](../../issues/new) and include the details below |
| **Request a feature** | [Open a feature request](../../issues/new) describing what you'd like and why |
| **Ask a question** | Search [existing issues](../../issues) first, then open a new one |
| **Report something private** (security, account, or safety) | See [Security & private reports](#security--private-reports) — please don't open a public issue |

Before opening a new issue, **search [existing issues](../../issues)** — someone may have
already reported it, and you can add details to the existing thread instead.

---

## Reporting a bug

Good bug reports get fixed faster. Please include:

1. **What happened** and **what you expected** to happen instead.
2. **Steps to reproduce** — the exact taps that lead to the problem.
3. **Device & OS** — e.g. *iPhone 15, iOS 18.2* or *Pixel 8, Android 15*.
4. **App version** — find it in **Settings** inside the app.
5. **A screenshot or screen recording**, if the problem is visual.

If the app crashes, note what you were doing the moment it closed — that's usually the
key detail.

## Requesting a feature

The Mud Room is built around a few intentional ideas (works-in-progress over finished
work, encouragement over likes, no forced face photos). Feature requests that fit that
spirit are very welcome.

When you open a request, tell us:

- **What you want to do** that you can't do today.
- **Why it matters** — the underlying goal, not just the proposed button. That helps us
  find the best solution, which is sometimes different from the first idea.

## FAQ

**Is The Mud Room available on iPhone and Android?**
It's designed for both. The app is built with Expo / React Native and ships as native
iOS and Android apps.

**How do I share a work-in-progress?**
Use the compose button in the **Mud Room** tab to post a photo of what you're working on,
with a short caption. The Mud Room is for *progress*, not polished, finished pieces.

**Why is there no "like" button?**
By design. The supportive action here is **Encourage** — deliberate product language for
a community built around momentum and support rather than approval metrics.

**Do I have to use a photo of my face as my avatar?**
No. Your avatar is your choice of representation — a sample of your work, a craft symbol
or monogram, or a photo. There are no forced face photos.

**How do practice reminders work?**
The Home tab tracks your sessions and can send gentle, pace-aware reminders to help you
keep a rhythm. You can adjust or turn these off in **Settings**.

**Where is my data stored?**
Today your profile and practice sessions are stored **locally on your device**. See the
note below on privacy.

**The app won't load / is stuck on a blank screen.**
First, fully close and reopen the app. If that doesn't help, make sure you're on the
latest version, then [open a bug report](../../issues/new) with your device and OS.

**Will there be more features in the app soon?** Since this is currently a free app we make updates and improvements as quickly as possible and as much as our resources allow. In the future, paid features may allow us to add more features, speed, and capablities.

## Privacy

Your profile and practice sessions are currently stored locally on your device. As
shared/community features come online, posts you choose to publish to the Mud Room feed
become visible to other members. We'll keep this page and the in-app information updated
as that rolls out. If you have a specific privacy question, reach out via the private
channel below.

---

## Security & private reports

**Please do not open a public issue** for security vulnerabilities, account problems, or
anything involving someone's personal safety. Instead:

- If a [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) policy exists in this repository, follow it.
- Otherwise, use [GitHub private vulnerability reporting](../../security/advisories/new),
  or contact the maintainer directly.

We take these seriously and will respond as quickly as we can.

---

## Response expectations

This is an actively developed project, but support is **best-effort** — there's no
guaranteed response time. Clear, detailed reports (steps, device, version, screenshots)
get resolved fastest. Thanks for helping make The Mud Room better. 💛
