# Privacy Policy — The Mud Room

Last updated: June 16, 2026

The Mud Room is a mobile app for creative practice, gentle accountability, and sharing
works-in-progress with a supportive community. This policy explains what information the
app handles, how it is used, and what choices you have.

This policy applies to The Mud Room mobile app for iOS and Android.

## Summary

- The current app stores your profile, goals, practice sessions, notes, reminder
  settings, and captured photos locally on your device.
- The current app does not sell personal information, run advertising, or use
  third-party advertising trackers.
- Camera access is optional and is used only when you choose to take a profile or
  progress photo.
- Notifications are optional and are used only for practice reminders and celebration
  messages.
- If you choose to publish a work-in-progress through community features, the content
  you publish is shared with other members.

## Information You Provide

The Mud Room may handle the following information when you enter it in the app:

- Profile details, such as your name, craft, optional location, profile picture, goals,
  and current project.
- Practice session details, such as the date of a logged session, the goal it relates
  to, an optional note, and an optional progress photo.
- Reminder preferences, such as whether reminders are enabled and the times of day you
  prefer.
- Community content, when available and when you choose to publish it, such as a
  work-in-progress image, caption, craft tag, display name, and profile information shown
  with the post.
- Support requests or private reports you choose to send, including any contact
  information and details you include in that message.

The app does not require a face photo. Your profile picture can be a bundled art image,
a work sample, a symbol, or a photo you choose.

## Camera, Photos, and Notifications

The Mud Room asks for camera permission only when you use a camera feature, such as
taking a profile picture or adding a progress photo to a practice update. Captured
photos are copied into the app's local storage. The app does not request microphone
access.

The current app does not request access to your full photo library. If a future version
adds photo-library selection, that permission will be requested separately by the
operating system.

Practice reminders are optional. If you enable them, the app uses your device's
notification system to schedule local reminders and celebration messages. You can turn
reminders off in the app or in your device settings.

## How Information Is Stored

In the current app, profile details, practice sessions, reminder preferences, and
captured photos are stored on your device. They are not sent to The Mud Room servers.
They may remain on your device until you clear your profile, delete the app, or remove
the app's stored data through your device settings.

Your device operating system may back up app data according to your iCloud, Google, or
device-backup settings. Those backups are controlled by your device and account
settings, not by The Mud Room.

If community posting or account features are enabled in your version of the app, content
you choose to publish may be stored on The Mud Room's servers or storage providers so it
can be shown to other members. Published content may remain available until you delete it
or ask us to remove it, unless we need to keep limited records for safety, security,
legal, or abuse-prevention reasons.

## How We Use Information

The Mud Room uses information handled by the app to:

- Show your Home tab, goals, practice progress, streaks, and current project.
- Save your profile and practice history on your device.
- Display profile pictures and progress photos you choose to add.
- Schedule optional practice reminders and celebration messages.
- Show community posts, profiles, and member information when you choose to publish or
  participate in shared features.
- Respond to support, safety, privacy, or security requests.
- Maintain, protect, debug, and improve the app.

We do not use your information for third-party advertising. We do not sell your personal
information.

## Sharing

We do not share your local profile, goals, practice notes, reminder settings, or local
progress photos with other members or with The Mud Room servers in the current app.

We may share information in the following limited situations:

- With other members, when you choose to publish community content or profile
  information intended to be visible in shared areas of the app.
- With service providers that help operate the app, such as app distribution, app update,
  hosting, storage, or support providers. These providers may process information only as
  needed to provide their services.
- With platform providers, such as Apple, Google, and Expo, as needed to distribute,
  update, secure, or operate the app. Their handling of information is governed by their
  own policies and your device settings.
- When required by law, legal process, or a valid government request.
- When necessary to protect the rights, safety, security, or integrity of The Mud Room,
  its members, or the public.

## Third-Party Services

The app is built with Expo and React Native and may contact Expo services for app
updates and related app-operation infrastructure. The app is distributed through Apple
and Google platforms, which may collect diagnostics, crash reports, purchase/download
records, or other information according to your platform settings and their privacy
policies.

The current app does not include advertising SDKs or third-party advertising trackers.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Edit your profile, goals, current project, and reminder settings in the app.
- Decline camera permission or notifications.
- Remove camera or notification permission later in your device settings.
- Use a bundled art image or symbol instead of a photo.
- Clear your local profile and practice data by using the app's start-over option.
- Delete the app to remove its local data from your device, subject to any device backups
  you control.
- Delete published community content or contact us to request help removing it, if shared
  posting features are available in your version of the app.

## Children

The Mud Room is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal
information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal
information through the app, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures for the information the app
handles. No app, device, network, or storage system is perfectly secure. You should avoid
posting or saving information in the app that you would not want others to see if your
device, account, or a shared community area were accessed by someone else.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as The Mud Room changes. If we make material changes, we will
update the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, provide additional notice in the
app or through the app store listing.

## Contact

For privacy questions, deletion requests, security reports, or other private concerns,
use the private reporting channel listed in the app's support information or app store
listing. Please do not include private information in a public issue report.
